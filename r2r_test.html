Travel from London to Paris

As two of the most popular tourist destinations in the world, the London to Paris journey is best done via Eurostar. The direct train ride from London St Pancras to Paris Nord station takes 2 hours 15 minutes, with 14 trains departing daily. A direct flight is your next best option, although that takes around double the duration once you factor in check-in times, and train travel to the city centre.
